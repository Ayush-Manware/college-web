A CENTER OF ART AND CULTURE

"Our college has societies for all major art forms"

DANCE SOCIETY

Instructing students to express a story with dance. Students learn
classical, western and regional dance forms
from experts and maestros.

MUSIC SOCIETY

The music society makes university come alive every evening
through there musical events and concerts. Society has many clubs
where students can learn FOLK LORE, INDIAN INSTRUMENTAL & VOCAL,
WESTERN INSTRUMENTAL & VOCAL.

LITERARY SOCIETY

This society caters to students who are interested in writing poetry and
prose. The society conducts various workshops as well as competitions
in order to groom budding authors.

ARTS SOCIETY

University students join this society to learn conceptual, sculpture
and paint arts. Their work finds its way on university walls and adds to the
aesthetic beauty.

THEATRE

This Club provides students with the opportunities to participate not only in
University plays, dramas, mime, mimicry, skits, etc; but also to compete
against the universities on state and national levels.
